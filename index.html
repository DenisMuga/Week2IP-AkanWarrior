About this Application

This a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. Most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born. Luckily using this application, they can know the day they were born corresponding to their Akan name convention.
